Donor & Recipient Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to ensure safe, ethical, and responsible blood donation.

Donor Guidelines

01

Age & Weight: Donors must be 18–60 years old and weigh at least 45 kg.

02

Health Status: Donors should be healthy and free from infectious diseases.

03

Donation Interval: Maintain a minimum gap of 3–4 months between donations.

04

Voluntary Act: Blood donation must be completely voluntary and unpaid.

05

Accurate Information: Provide correct blood group, contact details, and availability.

Recipient Guidelines

06

Medical Verification: Always verify blood group and cross-match at the hospital.

07

Respect Privacy: Donor information must only be used for emergency needs.

08

No Harassment: Donors must not be pressured or harassed in any way.

09

Genuine Requests: Fake or misleading requests will lead to account suspension.

10

Platform Responsibility: DonateLife only connects donors and recipients.

Our Mission & Vision

DonateLife is committed to saving lives through safe, voluntary, and timely blood donation. Our mission is to connect donors with those in urgent need, ensuring that help reaches everyone—everywhere.

Our vision is to build a compassionate and aware society where blood donation becomes a regular act of kindness, and no life is lost due to the unavailability of blood.

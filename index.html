Our Mission

To save lives by providing safe, voluntary, and timely blood donation. We connect donors with those in need, ensuring help reaches every person who requires it.

Our Vision

To create a compassionate and aware society where no life is lost due to lack of blood, and blood donation becomes a simple, everyday act of kindness.

Recent Events

Give Blood for Free,
Give Life with Love.

The Free Blood Donation Campaign at Yasin High School, Rajbari, organized in collaboration with the Bangladesh Red Crescent Society (BDRCS), Red Crescent Unit Rajbari, and Youth Red Crescent Rajbari Unit, reflects a strong commitment to humanity and community service. Through voluntary blood donation, this initiative aims to save lives, strengthen local blood reserves, and inspire compassion among the people of Rajbari.

Every Drop Counts — Together We Save Lives.

The Blood Donation Campaign 2025 reflects a shared commitment to compassion and public health. Organized through the collaboration of SCMST and Quantum Foundation Bangladesh, the initiative united students and staff in a collective effort to strengthen blood reserves and uphold humanitarian values. This campaign stands as a testament to responsible healthcare leadership and service to the community.

The Blood Donation Campaign organized by the Road Development Authority reflects our commitment beyond infrastructure—towards humanity. By coming together to donate blood, we are not only connecting roads but also connecting lives. A small act today can become someone’s lifeline tomorrow.
